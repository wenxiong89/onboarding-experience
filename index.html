Welcome!

We know you’re excited to learn, hone your skills and tackle some challenging problems. You’re probably wondering, “Great, where do I start?! Throw it all at me!” That’s the spirit - welcome to the club, fellow studioer.

Below are some steps you need to take to maintain momentum going into your first day at IBM Design.

02.

Decide

Who's helping you move, where are we mailing your info, and who's saving you when the caffeine can't?

Relocation

(Yay moving party!) If you’re relocating, IBM’s trusted partner, Graebel, will contact you. If you haven’t heard from them yet, remain calm. It can take up to two weeks from the time you accepted your offer for them to reach out.

Mailing Address

Where do you want us to send you your first big paycheck? Have an address lined up to put that money in the bank. Cha-Ching!

Emergency Contact

Great food and good company might save you in theory... But when reality calls, who do we need to contact?

03.

Prepare

Paying you is a priority and these are the things we'll need Day 1 to make it happen.

Passport

If you don’t have your passport, you’ll need at least two of three things: driver's license, birth certificate or Social Security card.

Proof of Graduation

If you graduated within the past two years we’ll need an official transcript from your Alma Mater (or a note from your Mother).

Banking Info

If having checks mailed to you isn’t your thing, have your bank routing number available so you can start bringing home the bacon.
