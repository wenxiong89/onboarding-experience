Welcome!

We know you’re excited to learn, hone your skills and tackle some challenging problems. You’re probably wondering, “Great, where do I start?! Throw it all at me!” That’s the spirit - welcome to the club, fellow studioer.

Below are some steps you need to take to maintain momentum going into your first day at IBM Design.

Things You Should Do Now

Identify

Benefits

IBM Benefits rock and we can’t wait to take care of you! We know that the current brochure can be confusing and in need of some serious Design Thinking.

Review IBM’s Benefit Options
Taxes

Prepare for Tax Forms by understanding your deductions (W4). We know it’s not fun…

Review IBM’s Benefit Options

Decide

Relocation

(Yay moving party!) If you’re relocating, IBM’s third-party partner, Graebel, will contact you. If you haven’t heard from them yet, remain calm. It can take up to two weeks from the time you accepted. If two weeks have already gone by, reach out to Cat (cfincun@us.ibm.com) and we’ll get things taken care of.

Mailing Address

Where do you want us to send you your first big paycheck? Have an address lined up to put that money in the bank. Cha-Ching!

Emergency Contact

Who’s going to save you when the caffeine can’t? Prepare emergency contacts with names and numbers

Bring These on Day 1

Passport

If you don’t have your passport, you’ll need your License, Birth Certificate and Social Security Card. Two of Three, please.

Proof of Graduation

If you graduated within the past two years we’ll need an Official Transcript from your Alma Mater (or a note from your Mother).

Banking Info

If having checks mailed to you isn’t your thing, have your bank routing number available… so you can start bringing home the bacon.
